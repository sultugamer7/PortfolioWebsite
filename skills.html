Think of a number

Think of a number game is all about guessing the number that the computer has thought of.

The rules are simple:

  • Guess a number.
  • Type the guessed number inside the number box.
  • Press the Guess button.
  • If your guess is right then you've won the game!
  • If your guess is not right then you'll get a hint if your guess is too high or too low.
  • Consider this hint and guess again until you've won.
  • Press the Restart button to play again.
Hangman

Hangman game is all about guessing the phrase that the computer has thought of.

The rules are simple:

  • Press the Start button to start the game.
  • You're given a phrase to guess and you've 8 lives.
  • Guess a letter in the phrase.
  • Press the guessed letter on your keyboard.
  • If your guess is right then the corresponding dashes will be replaced with the guessed letter.
  • If your guess is wrong then you'll lose a life.
  • If you've guessed the complete phrase then you've won the game!
  • If you've lost all the lives then you've lost the game!
  • Press the Restart button to restart the game.
